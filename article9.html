Content Delivery Networks (CDN) has low reach. Buffering issues are common, and downloads are slow in remote locations. Theta Fuel (TFUEL) is an operational token on the Theta blockchain and is used as payment for video content relayers' work, for viewing video content, creating and interacting with smart contracts and THETA staking. The emission of TFUEL tokens is unlimited but will occur at a rate of no more than 5% of the total supply of tokens per year. TFUEL's price history Theta Fuel token was launched in March 2019. The first major crypto exchange to list it was the South Korean Upbit, where TFUEL was traded in a pair with the South Korean won.
